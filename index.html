The Gratuitous Gram

July 12th 2014

9a - 1p

Easton Farmers Market

Center Square, Easton PA

Mixing 46g of crisco, 55g of white sugar and 63g of protein powder, despite what nutritional labeling might suggest, does not create a tilapia dinner.

While technically edible, the result would never be mistaken for the real dish. Why then does the nutrition label act as the basis for our understanding of health and food?

At The Gratuitous Gram, we will be reconstructing iconic dishes using the nutrition facts labels as recipes in order to start a conversation about how we all view food and nutrition.
